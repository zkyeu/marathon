Part 4 Reading Comprehension1

1. Why were the people in the countryside poor? Because the place was very___________

  • A. dry
  • B. windy
  • C. hot
  • D. water

Frank was only 5 years old. He lived with his parents in a very small house in countryside. The people there were all poor because it is very dry there.

One night, when everybody was asleep, Peter heard some noise. It came out from the kitchen. He found that the wood in the kitchen was burning. There was no water in the house, so he could not put out the fire. He shouted loudly to wake up everyone in the house. Then he ran out of his house and knocked on the doors of his neighbors to wake them up. Fortunately, all his neighbors left their houses quickly.

At last, the fire was put out by the firemen. Many houses were burnt. But nobody was hurt in the fire.
