51TALK JOINS 9TH INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE
ON TEACHER EDUCATION

by Amanda · October 21th

Happy Moment

51Talk was on hand during the 9th International Conference on Teacher Education held at the GT Toyota Auditorium-Asian Center, University of the Philippines Diliman, Quezon City, on September 1, 2016.

51Talk Marketing Director Mikel Canete discussed how 51Talk, as the leading online English teaching platform, provides comprehensive English education to Chinese learners through its unique teaching model of one student to one teacher. He also talked about Filipinos as the perfect choice for 51Talk’s teaching opportunities, and 51Talk being listed publicly on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE: COE) in June of this year.
